My research examines how family processes and parenting experiences are linked to adolescent psychological and social development.

Parenting and Parental Burnout
======

I study how parental burnout relates to family cohesion, parent-child relationships, adolescents' internet gaming addiction, and bullying-related outcomes.

Family Dynamics
======

My work considers family systems, parent-child relationships, and differences between parental roles as developmental contexts for adolescents.

Adolescent Mental Health
======

I am interested in adolescent mental health, bullying victimization, prosocial behavior, self-esteem, and school-related adjustment.

Methods
======

My current work uses longitudinal designs and structural equation modeling approaches to examine developmental processes over time.
